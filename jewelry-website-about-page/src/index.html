About

Here's a summary about us:

    We are a jewelry merchant incorporated as an LLC specializing in the purchase and resale of jewelry. Our catalog of jewlry will fascinate even the most stingest of buyers, and anyone can place a bid on any of our offerings! The highest bidder wins. Everyone gets a chance, no one ever loses out!

    We also have great deals for your unwanted jewelry! You are always welcome to submit an inquiry and we are more than happy to evaluate and appraise your jewelry and come up with an approximate price we will buy it for! We offer additional raises on jewelry bundles.

    If you're instead interested in a business partnership, also welcome to submit an inquiry! We are a fast-growing company and are always open to new potential partnerships with existing firms.

    This company was founded in 2023 by Kevin Zhang, a software engineer at Raytheon Technologies. He works full time, but spends his part time on this side hustle. You are welcome to reach out to him at 617-584-4607 if you have any questions or inquires. He holds a bachelor's degree in computer science from the University of Massachusetts Lowell and is currently getting his master's degree in systems engineering from John Hopkins university.
